Multiple apps, single platform

We know how important it is to have easy access to the information needed to run your business. That's why our Tecxprt Accounts apps were designed from the ground up to work together seamlessly.

Real-Time Analytics & Tracking

Tecxprt Accounts provides real-time updates of all your orders and inventory and is the essential business management software your business needs for maximum efficiency.

Features

Invoicing

We built Tecxprt Accounts to let you create professional looking invoices with just the click of a button. With simple, editable invoice templates, you can easily add your company logo, change fonts and colors, and even attach pictures.

Order fulfillment

Order fulfillment helps you to manage your order by storing it in our cloud-based warehouse. Your orders' data is pushed to us as soon as it changes on your website and we automatically apply any.

Purchase Orders

Tecxprt is an easy to use application that automates the creation, approval and issuance of purchase orders. This saves time and can even save money for both your company and your supplier.

Vendor Portal

Tecxprt makes it simple for you to check on your existing supplier agreements, view purchase orders, and more. Just log in with the username and password provided with your invitation to Vendor Portal.

Banking

Manage all you spends through our banking module. Credits, debits, movements, payments, all it takes to manage your everyday expenses..

Account Statements

With Tecxprt Accounts, you can view all your supplier account statement at one single place, improve the relationship with your suppliers by understanding their payment performance and help them manage risk through credit alerts based on your invoices.
